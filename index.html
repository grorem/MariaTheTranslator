I was born in Zalău, Transilvania, Romania, where I lived until the age of 14. Then, my family and I moved to Barcelona, Spain where I continued my studies. I graduated from Translation and Interpreting at Universitat Autònoma de Barcelona and one year later from the Biomedical and Pharmaceutical Translation Master at Pompeu Fabra University (IDEC).

I was not born in Spain, that is true. However, due to the nature of my studies and professional carrier, I consider myself being a bilingual native Spanish and Romanian speaker.

Languages have always been my passion and at the age of 18 I know I would definitely go for Translation studies. During the 4 years of the program I was able to get in touch with different fields such as literary, financial, legal and technical translation but my favorite was, undoubtedly, the technical and scientific content. After finishing the degree, I decided I should complement my education in order to be able to offer an accurate, precise and true reflection of the source texts I was going to translate.
